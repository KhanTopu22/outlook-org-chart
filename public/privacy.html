Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Outlook Org Chart" add-in ("we", "our", or "the Add-in"), provided by Tanvir Khan, collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The Outlook Org Chart add-in is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or organizational data on our servers. We operate no backend server that receives your data.

All data processed by the Add-in — including organizational charts, contact information (names, emails, titles), uploaded files, and settings (such as Favorites and Recently Used contacts) — remains locally on your device, in your browser's localStorage.

Any file you upload is read and processed entirely within your browser; its contents are not transmitted anywhere.

2. Data Usage

The Add-in reads the contact information you explicitly provide (via manual entry or file upload) and uses it solely to display the organizational chart and to assist you in selecting email recipients (To/Cc) within your Outlook client.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because all data is stored locally, you remain in full control of it. You can delete it at any time by clearing the Favorites/Recently Used lists within the Add-in, clearing your browser's site data, or uninstalling the Add-in. Removing the Add-in or clearing browser storage permanently deletes any data it held on your device.

4. Third-Party Access

Because we do not collect or store your data on external servers, we do not share, sell, or rent any of your personal information to third parties. Your Microsoft 365 data remains subject to the agreement between you (or your organization) and Microsoft.

5. Security

While the Add-in does not transmit your data externally, the security of locally stored data depends on the security of your device, browser, and Microsoft 365 account. We recommend keeping these protected and up to date.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
