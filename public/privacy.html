Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Outlook Org Chart" add-in ("we", "our", or "the Add-in") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The Outlook Org Chart add-in is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or organizational data on our servers.

All data processed by the Add-in, including organizational charts, contact information (names, emails, titles), and settings (such as Favorites and Recently Used contacts), is stored exclusively and locally within your device's browser using `localStorage` or within your own Microsoft 365 environment.

2. Data Usage

The Add-in only reads the contact information you explicitly provide (via manual entry or file upload) and uses it solely to display the organizational chart and assist you in selecting email recipients (To/Cc) within your Outlook client.

3. Third-Party Access

Because we do not collect or store your data on external servers, we do not share, sell, or rent any of your personal information to third parties.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

5. Contact Us
